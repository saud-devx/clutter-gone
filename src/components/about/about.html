ABOUT US

Clutter-b-gone originated from my passion to help and transform people’s lives by using my expertise in decluttering and organising spaces to help clients reclaim their space and simplify their lives.

Having worked in public services helping others for 15 years, I decided the time was right to start my business in professional organising so I could continue doing what I love! Helping people. Over the last 15 years, I have managed multiple projects, organised events and worked with people to achieve their goals which has enabled me to define my skill set as a professional organiser and ensures I am the right person to help you. I work with my clients to understand what you want to achieve, understand your space and belongings and understand any concerns you may have. This is done through a FREE initial 30 minute one on one consultation. This allows me to truly understand the best way forward to help you and design a bespoke plan for my clients.

I can work with you or without you to organise your spaces, rooms and homes to ensure your spaces are functional, comfortable and clutter free. I can help working professionals who don’t have the time to declutter, families and students who need to create functional and spacious environments, individuals who are overwhelmed with clutter and need help knowing where to start and I work extremely closely with homeowners who struggle with hoarding, allowing the time and sensitivity that is needed to start taking steps to a clutter free life.

I am proud to offer discounts on my pricing so the more time you invest in decluttering and organising your spaces, the cheaper it becomes.
